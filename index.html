About the Event

Sankhya is the annual cultural fest organised by the BS in Analytics and Sustainability Studies at Tata Institute of Social Sciences Mumbai. Rooted in the theme for this year “Circle of Life”, Sankhya celebrates the idea of a Circular Economy, where growth, creativity, and responsibility flow together in harmony.
